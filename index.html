1992

On June 1st, 1992, UNIBEN was founded from the predecessor company named Viet Hung Food Industry Company Limited, mainly producing instant noodles.

2004

Launching brand "3 Miền" aiming at domestic market with instant noodle as the leading product. Since then, we have never ceased to diversify the product portfolio to tap into various consumer demands.

2009

With the aim of diversifying products to better meet the consumer's needs and contribute to community health improvement, we have collaborated with Ho Chi Minh City Nutrition Center to launch 3 Miền I-diode supplement seasoning products.

2012

REEVA brand was invented to help propel us towards international market penetration.

2013

Launch 3 Miền Fish Sauce made with the secret technique of fresh and delicious fish sauce to easily prepare dishes including stews, soups, or dipping sauces with a rich and authentic flavor.

2014

We renamed to UNIBEN Joint Stock Company, with the goal of providing high-quality, delicious, and nutritious products to both domestic and international market.

2015

We inaugurated a new UNIBEN factory in Hung Yen province, which is designed in compliance with modern European standards. The factory manufactures instant noodles and seasonings to meet the timely demands of the market in the North and Central North regions.

2016

"3 Miền" brand is one of the "Most Chosen Brands Food Vietnam in 2016” according to Kantar Worldpanel, a leading global market research company.

2018

UNIBEN is crowned winner of ‘Health Promotion’ in the Asia Responsible Enterprise Awards in 2018 (AREA) for 3 Miền iodine supplemented seasoning, which contributes to reduce iodine deficiency in the community.

2019

We inaugurated the second UNIBEN factory in Binh Duong province, which is designed in compliance with European, modern standards with a total investment of 1,000 billion VND.

2019

Basing on the success in food industry, UNIBEN decided to expand into beverage industry with an innovative product "JOCO Fruit Juice Drink - Creatively Blended from Nature".

2020

Following the successful momentum of JOCO Fruit Juice Drink, UNIBEN has introduced BONCHA Honey Tea "Fresh Fushion" made from 100% pure green tea leaves and honey, which helps quench your thirst and excites your senses.

2022

3 Miền Instant Noodle is the “Most choosen packaged brand" in rural area in 6 years from 2017-2022, according to Kantar Worldpanel.

2022

UNIBEN has marked a new stage in the beverage category by introducing a brand new innovative energy drink named ABBEN, aiming at young people and positioning as the "Accurate energy standard for power generation."
